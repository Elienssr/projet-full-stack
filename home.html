Evergreen Market

Evergreen Online Supermarket was launched in 2015 with a vision to revolutionize grocery shopping. Evergreen aim to bring convenience and sustainability to customers' doorsteps. Starting with locally sourced fresh produce and pantry essentials, the platform quickly expanded to include a wide range of products, from organic items to household necessities. What set Evergreen apart was its commitment to eco-friendly packaging and fast delivery, ensuring quality while reducing its environmental footprint. Today, it’s a go-to platform for thousands, offering a seamless shopping experience that saves time while supporting local farmers and businesses.
